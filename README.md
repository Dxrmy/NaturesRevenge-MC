**Nature's Revenge** turns the Minecraft environment itself into your greatest adversary. It adds survival mechanics that force players to respect the natural world—or suffer catastrophic consequences.

## Features
- **Aggressive Flora:** Thorny vines, poisonous plants, and biome-specific vegetation that reacts dynamically to player presence.
- **Meteorological Hazards:** New weather events (acid rain, heatwaves) that impact survival, entity behavior, and block stability.
- **Environmental Status Effects:** Biome-native hazards that modify health regeneration and movement speed.
- **Hardcore-First Balancing:** Specifically tuned for players who want a steeper, high-stakes survival curve.
